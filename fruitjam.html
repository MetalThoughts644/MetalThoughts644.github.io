Fruit Jam

  • Vegan
  • Sweet

Jam made with any type of fruit. Berries require some apple as well.

Cover the bottom of the saucepan with water

  • 4 parts fruit
  • 3 parts sugar

Boil fruit until happy with consistancy. Mix sugar in

Boil/simmer until jam sets

Enjoy!
