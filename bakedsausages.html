Baked Sausages

  • Meat

Preheat oven to 180C

  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp dry mustard
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Mix all ingredients together.

  • 500g beef sausages

Spoon mixture over sausages

Bake for 20-25 minutes

Enjoy!
