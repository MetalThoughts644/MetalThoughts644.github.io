* `100g` butter
* `75g` plain flour
* `25g` desiccated coconut
* `50g` Demerara sugar
* `25g` flaked almonds

>Soften butter then rub in flour, coconut, sugar and almonds. 

---

>Spread over dish and bake in 180C for 30mins or until golden.
