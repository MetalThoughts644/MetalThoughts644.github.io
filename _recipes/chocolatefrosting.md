* 60g butter

>Cream the butter

---

* 1 cup icing sugar
* 3 tbsp cocoa

>Sift together the sugar & cocoa, gradually beat into butter

---

* 4 tsp milk

>Beat in milk until light & fluffy

---

>Spread over cold cake
