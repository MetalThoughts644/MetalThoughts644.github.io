>Preheat oven to 200C

---

* `500g` chicken mince
* `1` shallot
* `1/2 cup` panko breadcrumbs
* `1/4 cup` parmesan, grated
* `1/2 cup` basil pesto
* `1` egg
* `1/4 tsp` salt
* `1/4 tsp` pepper

>Mix all ingrediants together in a bowl. Form into meatball shapes on a lined baking tray. 

---

>Bake for 20mins or until done
