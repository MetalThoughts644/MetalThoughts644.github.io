* `6` chicken drumsticks
* `60g` butter
* `1 tbsp` tomato sauce
* `1 tbsp` soy sauce
* `1 tbsp` worsteshire sauce
* `1 tbsp` grated ginger
* `1 tbsp` brown sugar
* `1 tbsp` mustard
* salt and pepper

>Melt everything together and marinade the chicken in it for 1-2 hours

---

>Cook in oven at 180C fan forced for 45mins or until cooked through
