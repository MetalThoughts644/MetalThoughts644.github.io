Pizza Base

  • Vegan
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup tapioca flour
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil

Mix flours and oil in a bowl

  • 1/2 cup of warm water
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1 tbs yeast

In another bowl mix water, sugar, yeast. Leave to prove

Add yeast mix to flour. Add more warm water to mix to a custard type texture

Enjoy!
